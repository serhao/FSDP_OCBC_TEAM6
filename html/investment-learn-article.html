Your investment journey – why and how you should invest

February 2025 | By OCBC Singapore | 10 mins read

When it comes to securing your financial future, just saving stone may not be enough. In fact, starting to invest money investing is important and how you can get started with as little as S$$100 a month.

Part 1: Stretching your dollar – why you should invest

“To thine own self be true”, Hamlet, by William Shakespeare
Many of us look forward to when we can stop working and quit our day jobs and still be able to afford a comfortable life after that.
But before we start dreaming, let’s consider some cold, hard facts.
The cost of living is on the rise and no one knows better than us on where and what it costs. But whether it’s an occasional restaurant meal, commuting via taxis now and then or as little as a quick getaway – the cost of enjoying life will definitely increase over a such period of two or three decades.

While it can be a pleasant prospect of having to pay off huge bills at the end of each month and not worry about money, a lot of us end up living to the grindstone for a monthy pay cheque that we hope to stretch as much as possible.
Another crucial realization is that it will be a big challenge saving your way to riches over the longer term.
The only way to grow your wealth is by investing.
